Kundli Details

A Kundli, also known as a birth chart or horoscope, is an astrological chart or diagram that represents the positions of celestial bodies at the time of an individual's birth.A Janam Kundli is your stored karmas' of the previous birth.

Today Panchang

Panchang, also known as Panchangam, is a Hindu calendar that provides comprehensive astrological and astronomical information for a specific day. It includes details such as tithi (lunar day), nakshatra (lunar mansion), yoga (auspicious period).

Horoscope

Horoscopes can provide various aspects of a person's life, including relationships, career, health. They are typically presented as daily, weekly, monthly or yearly based on movement and alignment of celestial bodies in relation to the zodiac signs.

Chaughadiya Muhurta

Chaughadiya Muhurta, also known as Choghadiya or Choghadiya Muhurat, is a traditional system in Hindu astrology used to determine auspicious and inauspicious time periods during a day for various activities.

ASTROLOGY

Astrology is a method of predicting mundane events based upon the assumption that the celestial bodies—particularly the planets and the stars considered in their arbitrary combinations or configurations (called constellations) in some way either determine or indicate changes in the sublunar world.
